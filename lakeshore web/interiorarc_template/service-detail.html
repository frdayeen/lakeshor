Roofing

Residential and commercial roofing solutions provided for you, our customers. Since 3-tab style roof only provides 60 mph wind warranty, we recommend architectural style shingles with top of the line wind warranty and algae resistance. These products are chosen after robust product review and performance analysis. That’s why we prefer to use GAF and IKO, which pass our product review. You can choose from regular two color, multi-color with natural shadow, slate looking or shake looking asphalt shingles. We provide customers with options of proper ventilation and insulation before putting a new roof on.

  • Inspection with FLIR and moisture meter to explore preventive action.
  • Tear off the old roof and layers.
  • Inspection and repair/replacement of decking materials.
  • Ice and water at the eaves and valley(s), flashing around external components per manufacturer specifications
  • Synthetic deck protection
  • Starter, shingle and hip-ridge finishing.
  • Clean up and disposal.
  • Inspection after production.

Siding

You can choose from vinyl, fiber-cement, engineered wood, and cellular composite for your siding project needs and budget. Installation of 0.046” thick vinyl siding is recommended due to our commitment to quality. All our vinyl product are energy efficient compared to aluminum siding. Our upgraded products come with 1.75” hail resistance with virtually seamless look and great curb appeal. These products are chosen after robust product review and performance analysis. Our recommendation on vinyl siding is Royal Building Products, which passes our product review.

  • Inspection with FLIR and moisture meter to explore preventive action.
  • Tear off the old siding.
  • Synthetic house wrap.
  • Finishing with siding panel and trim.
  • Clean up and disposal.
  • Inspection after production.

Windows

Windows play a very important role for the energy efficiency of your home. Double hung, casement, awning, picture, bay and custom windows are available to upgrade. Lansing products are our main supplier for windows. For sliding and French door, we use ProVia and Windsor. Our windows are third party verified meeting energy star, AAMA and NFRC requirements. Customers can choose different screen, interior color, wood texture, grit option to meet their particular needs.

  • Inspection with FLIR to find out the leak and other problems.
  • Taking off old window.
  • Finishing with new window matching the capping.
  • Clean up and disposal.
  • Inspection with FLIR after production and operation demonstration.
