Your roofing and siding needs

Lakeshore Builders Restoration is a friends and family owned company based in Elgin, IL. It all started in 2016, when two friends (Juan and Jorge) with 15 years of combined remodeling experience, decided to become partners to serve the community better and grow using their specific skillsets. Since then, three new divisions, besides restoration, have been launched – retail, consultancy and R&D. We believe in technical sales process, educating our customers and employees. We have an entire program for community outreach and we take pride in restoring one of your most valuable assets, your home.
